Technology

The technology is scalable by modules. This makes 3C suitable for poins of emission from 10.000 MT / year to 1.000.000 MT / year based on a couple of standardized plant sizes. The containerized standard modules allows for a simplified Hook-Up and costsaving on Civil Works. Due to its solvent neutrality, it is possible to use solvent tailormade for the specific point of emission. We are in collaboration with several solvent providers.
