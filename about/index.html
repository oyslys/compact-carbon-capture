About us

The 3C technology is invented in a collaboration with Fjell Technology Group AS, Equinor ASA, CMR Prototech AS and SINTEF Tel-Tek. The development project was initiated by Equinor back in 2007, but the first patent for the technology (by Prof. Dag Eimer) was granted already in 1999. Today, the company has their headquarter at Marineholmen in Bergen,Norway, but collaborates with R&D partners and industrial partners both nationally and globally.
